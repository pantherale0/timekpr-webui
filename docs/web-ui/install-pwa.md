# Installing Guardian on Your Phone

Guardian can be added to your phone’s home screen for quick access to routines, family dialogue, and household settings — like a regular app, without visiting the app store.

## Android (Chrome)

1. Sign in to Guardian in Chrome on your phone.
2. When the **Add Guardian to your home screen** banner appears at the bottom, tap **Install**.
3. If you do not see the banner, open the browser menu (⋮) and choose **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.

## iPhone and iPad (Safari)

Safari does not show a one-tap install button. Use Apple’s share menu instead:

1. Sign in to Guardian in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Confirm the name **Guardian**, then tap **Add**.

Guardian opens in full-screen mode from your home screen, separate from the browser tab bar.

## Tips

- Install prompts appear on mobile-sized screens and can be dismissed for seven days.
- After installing, open Guardian from the home screen icon — not an old browser bookmark — for the best experience.
- Guardian keeps admin data fresh: only the app shell is cached offline; your family data always loads from the server when connected.
